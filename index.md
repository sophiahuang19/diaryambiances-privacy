# Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026

DiaryAmbiances is designed as a local-first diary app. This policy describes the current prototype/release direction for the iOS app.

## Data We Collect

DiaryAmbiances does not currently collect personal data from users.

The app does not currently:

- create user accounts
- upload diary entries to a server
- use third-party analytics SDKs
- track users across apps or websites
- sell user data
- collect precise location
- access contacts, photos, microphone, camera, or health data

## Diary Content

Diary entries are stored locally on the user's device. The app uses local device storage for diary text, timestamps, selected writing mode, and related entry metadata.

Diary content is not transmitted to DiaryAmbiances servers in the current version.

## App Preferences

The app may store local preferences on the device, such as whether the welcome screen has been seen and the selected background audio volume.

These preferences remain on the device unless the user deletes the app or the operating system removes app data.

## Audio And Visual Assets

The app includes bundled fonts, background images, and ambience audio files. These assets are used only to render the app experience. Playing background ambience does not record or analyze user audio.

## Third-Party Services

The current app does not include third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Audio assets were generated externally during development, but generated audio files are bundled into the app. The app itself does not call ElevenLabs or other generation services at runtime.

## Children

DiaryAmbiances is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes To This Policy

If future versions add accounts, cloud sync, analytics, AI features, playlist integrations, or any server-side processing, this policy and the App Store privacy details must be updated before release.
